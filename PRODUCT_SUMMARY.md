# Riskily SME — Product Summary

## What We're Building

Riskily SME is a **financial risk intelligence platform for small and medium enterprises in Kenya**. It gives SME owners a single dashboard that translates complex financial and regulatory risk into a plain health score (0–100) across seven business risk modules — updated daily for paying subscribers, weekly on the free tier.

The platform is bilingual (English + Swahili), mobile-first, and designed to work on low-bandwidth connections. It is the SME-facing product of the broader Riskily risk analytics suite, stripped of the complexity that makes corporate treasury tools inaccessible to a business owner managing operations from a smartphone.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Kenyan SMEs operate in an unusually volatile environment:

- **CBK rate moves** ripple into credit costs and loan repayments overnight
- **USD/KES fluctuations** affect importers, exporters, and anyone holding dollar-denominated debt
- **Fuel and commodity prices** (petrol, unga) directly hit operational costs
- **Regulatory circulars from CBK, KRA, NSSF, NHIF** carry real penalties for late or missed compliance
- **Credit and liquidity pressure** can build silently until it becomes a cash crisis

Most SME owners have no system to monitor any of this. Accountants are reactive. Banks send generic notices. There is no affordable, real-time, Kenya-specific risk dashboard built for the business owner — not the CFO.

---

## The Seven Risk Modules

Each module blends the owner's **exposure** (derived from their onboarding profile) with live **market pressure** to produce a health score:

| Module | What It Tracks |
|---|---|
| **Credit** | Loan exposure vs CBR/T-Bill rate pressure |
| **Liquidity** | Cash runway vs credit availability |
| **FX** | USD/KES exposure from imports, exports, or dollar debt |
| **Macro** | General economic pressure (CBR, T-Bill, broad indicators) |
| **Commodity** | Fuel and food price exposure (petrol, unga) |
| **Regulatory** | Active CBK circulars; provisional health caps when circulars are pending |
| **Supply Chain** | Upstream/downstream dependency risk |

The **overall health score** is a weighted blend of all seven, tilted toward the modules where the owner has highest exposure.

## Core Features

**Health Score Gauge** — The single number every user sees first. Band-coded (All Good / Watch Out / Act Now / Urgent) with a description in plain language.

**Module Breakdown** — Drill into each of the seven modules to see what's driving the score, with exposure and pressure detail available to Standard subscribers.

**Alerts** — Push-style notifications routed by severity. Basic tier receives URGENT and ACT_NOW alerts only (capped at 3/month). Standard tier receives all severities, unlimited. Alerts fire when the scoring engine detects a band change or threshold breach.

**Compliance Deadline Grid** — Upcoming KRA, CBK, NSSF, NHIF, and KEBS deadlines with a days-remaining countdown and urgency colour coding. Basic users see the next 3; Standard users see the next 6.

**Market Data Strip** — Live USD/KES rate, CBK base rate, fuel price (KES/L), and maize flour price — refreshed daily at 06:00 Nairobi time. Contextualises the score rather than leaving the owner to guess what moved.

**Upgrade Path** — Locked Standard features show a real upgrade prompt (never a disabled button). The tier distinction is designed to let a business owner try the product for free and understand exactly what they gain by paying.

## Who It's For

An SME owner in Kenya — a retailer, importer, manufacturer, or service business — who:
- Has some exposure to credit, FX, or commodity markets
- Must comply with KRA, NSSF, NHIF, and CBK obligations
- Wants a single-screen answer to "is my business financially safe right now?"
- Is more likely to be on a mid-range Android phone on a 3G connection than a desktop

---

## The Goal

**Phase 1:** Deliver a working, accurate, mobile-ready risk dashboard that a Kenyan SME owner can sign up for, profile their business, and receive a real — not demo — health score with actionable alerts and compliance deadlines. No placeholder data. No fake metrics.

**Long-term:** Become the financial early-warning system that sits between a Kenyan SME and the next crisis it didn't see coming.
